How to Use This Tool
Quick reference for building and sharing MD onboarding schedules
Building a Schedule
1
Select your BU
Use the blue bar. Each BU stores its own service line contacts, MD roster, onboarding dates, and schedule template.
2
Input tab
Enter MD name, BU, service line, start date, city, EA, PM, mentor. Contacts auto-fill. The 2026 A&M onboarding dates are pre-loaded and auto-match within 28 days of start date.
3
Generate Schedule
Click Generate. Builds a full schedule from the BU template. SL MDs from the roster appear as individual rows (First Last). BU name replaces [BU] in titles like “CPI Finance Overview.”
4
Fill dates & times
Right-click any Date or Time cell and choose Fill Down to copy it to empty rows below in that week. Standard Ctrl+C / Ctrl+V also works in any cell.
5
Save the schedule
Click Save Schedule to store this MD's plan. Saved plans appear under Saved Schedules, grouped by BU / Region.
6
Print View
Print View produces a clean PDF you can save or share.
Blue border = auto-filled · Amber = manual · Saves automatically
